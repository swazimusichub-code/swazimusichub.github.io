Imibuto Levamise Kubutwa (Frequently Asked Questions)

How do I create an account? ▼

Click the "Sign In" button in the top right corner, then select "Sign Up". You can register using your email address or through Facebook, Google, or Apple.

I forgot my password. What should I do? ▼

On the sign-in page, click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

How do I upload my music? ▼

After signing in as an artist, go to your Artist Dashboard and click "Upload Music". You'll need to provide audio files, album artwork, and track information.

How much do artists earn? ▼

Artists earn 70% of all revenue generated from their music. Payments are made monthly when your balance exceeds E200.

How do I cancel my subscription? ▼

Go to Account Settings → Subscription → Cancel Subscription. Your premium features will continue until the end of your billing period.
