© Our Commitment / Sithembiso Setfu

© ™ ® 🎵

Swazi Music Hub is committed to protecting the rights of artists and copyright holders. This policy explains how we handle copyright issues and what to do if you believe your work has been used without permission.

📘 siSwati: Siyakuhlonipha nelilungelo lebaculi. Nangabe umsebenti wakho usetjentiswe ngaphandle kwemvumo, sitsintsa natsi.

We take copyright infringement seriously and respond to clear notices of alleged infringement. We have a policy of terminating, in appropriate circumstances, the accounts of users who are repeat infringers.

📋 DMCA Compliance

🌍 International Copyright Standards

While based in Eswatini, we comply with international copyright standards including the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) for our global users. We have designated an agent to receive notices of claimed infringement.

⚠️ Filing a Copyright Complaint / Kufaka Sicelo

If you believe your copyrighted work has been used on our platform without authorization, please provide our Copyright Agent with a written notice containing:

  1. Identification of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed
  2. Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing, with enough detail for us to locate it (URL, track title, artist name)
  3. Your contact information: name, address, phone number, and email
  4. A statement that you have a good faith belief that use is not authorized
  5. A statement that the information is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the owner
  6. Your physical or electronic signature
⚠️
Important: Section 512(f) of the DMCA provides that any person who knowingly materially misrepresents that material is infringing may be subject to liability. Please ensure your complaint is accurate.

↩️ Counter-Notification / Kuphikisa

If you believe your content was removed by mistake, you may file a counter-notification containing:

  • Identification of the material removed and its location before removal
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed by mistake
  • Your name, address, and phone number
  • A statement that you consent to jurisdiction of federal court in your district
  • Your signature
📨 Send counter-notifications to: copyright@swazimusichub.com with subject line "COUNTER-NOTIFICATION"

🎤 Artist Rights / Emalungelo Emculi

As an artist on Swazi Music Hub, you retain:

  • Ownership: You own your music and retain all rights to your work
  • Control: You decide where your music is available
  • Revenue: You earn royalties from streams and downloads
  • Credit: You are properly attributed for your work

By uploading music, you grant us a license to distribute your music through our platform and partner services. You can remove your music at any time.

📝 Remember: You retain 100% ownership of your music. We only have the rights necessary to distribute and promote your work on your behalf.

⚖️ Fair Use / Kusebentisa Lokufanele

Some uses of copyrighted material may be considered "fair use" under applicable laws, including:

  • Criticism and commentary - Reviews, critiques, and analysis
  • News reporting - Journalism and current events
  • Teaching and research - Educational purposes
  • Parody - Satire and comedic use

If you believe your use qualifies as fair use, please include that explanation in any communications with us.
