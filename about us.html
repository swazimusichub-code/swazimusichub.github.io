Lendzaba Yetfu

Our Story (Indzaba yetfu)

Swazi Music Hub was born from a simple observation: Swazi music, despite its richness and diversity, lacked a dedicated digital home. Traditional rhythms, contemporary beats, and the voices of Eswatini's talented artists were scattered across platforms, often losing their cultural context.

In 2023, a group of music lovers, tech entrepreneurs, and cultural preservationists came together with a vision. What if we could create a space where siSwati music isn't just streamed, but celebrated? Where artists get fair compensation, and listeners can discover the full spectrum of Swazi musical expression?

Today, that vision is reality. Swazi Music Hub has grown from a small startup to the premier destination for Swazi music, connecting artists with fans across Eswatini and around the globe. We're proud to be part of the story of Swazi music, and we're just getting started.

Our Mission

To empower Swazi artists with cutting-edge digital tools and global distribution channels while preserving the authenticity and richness of siSwati music for worldwide audiences. We believe that every artist deserves a platform that respects their craft and connects them with listeners who will appreciate their unique voice.
